Coffee Menu:

Espresso

A rich and intense coffee brewed by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans. Served as a small, strong shot.


Price: $3.00

Caffè Latte

Espresso mixed with steamed milk, topped with a light layer of foam for a creamy, smooth coffee experience.


Price: $4.50 to $5.00

Cappuccino

Equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam, often topped with a sprinkle of cocoa or cinnamon.


Price: $4.50 to $5.00

Americano

Espresso diluted with hot water for a lighter, smooth coffee, similar in strength to brewed coffee.


Price: $3.50

Mocha

A chocolate-flavored variant of a latte combining espresso, steamed milk, chocolate syrup, and sometimes whipped cream.


Price: $5.00 to $5.50

About The Scorch

At The Scorch, mornings aren’t just another part of the day – they’re where the magic begins.


Our coffee beans are roasted with the intensity of the rising sun and brewed to perfection, bringing you a cup that’s rich, bold, and full of life.


We believe coffee should do more than wake you up – it should ignite your day.


That’s why every sip here comes with warmth, energy, and a little bit of morning sunshine.


Beyond our drinks, you’ll find an array of fresh bakes and hearty bites, made daily in-house to pair perfectly with your cup. From buttery croissants to the crunch of biscotti, everything we serve is crafted with the same passion that goes into our coffee.


So whether you’re starting your workday, catching up with friends, or simply soaking in a quiet morning moment, The Scorch is your space to rise, shine… and sip.
